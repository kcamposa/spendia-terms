5. Apple Pay Integration (Optional)

5.1 Voluntary Feature

Apple Pay transaction tracking is completely optional and requires:

  • Manual setup of an iOS Shortcuts automation on your device
  • Your explicit consent to share transaction data with Spendia
  • An active Apple Pay account (managed by Apple, not Spendia)

5.2 How It Works

  • When you make an Apple Pay purchase, the iOS Shortcut sends transaction details (amount, merchant, date) to Spendia
  • Spendia uses AI to suggest a category based on the merchant name
  • This counts as one AI request toward your daily limit
  • You can edit or delete these entries at any time

5.3 Data from Apple Pay

  • We only receive data you explicitly share through the Shortcut
  • We do NOT have access to your Apple Pay account, payment methods, or full transaction history
  • We do NOT store receipt images or detailed transaction metadata from Apple Pay
  • You can disable this feature at any time by removing the iOS Shortcut
